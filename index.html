05 — Voice & Tone

Brand Voice

Momentify speaks with confidence, precision, and a forward-facing energy. We are the technology layer that makes experiences matter.

⚡
Energetic
We bring momentum and excitement. Language should feel alive, active, and dynamic — never passive or corporate.
🎯
Precise
Every word earns its place. We communicate clearly, cutting jargon in favour of language that's direct and meaningful.
🚀
Forward-Looking
We talk about what's possible, not what already exists. Tone is optimistic, ambitious, and technology-forward.

✓ Do

  • Use active voice: "Momentify captures every interaction"
  • Lead with outcomes: "Turn attendees into advocates"
  • Be specific: "Real-time data, zero latency"
  • Speak to transformation: "Before / After"
  • Use short, punchy headlines

✗ Don't

  • Use passive voice: "Data is captured by the platform"
  • Over-explain the technology stack
  • Use filler words: "very", "really", "just", "actually"
  • Sound like every other SaaS brand
  • Use exclamation marks excessively
06 — Usage

Logo Usage Rules

Consistent application of the logo builds trust and recognition. These rules are non-negotiable.

1

Clear Space

Always maintain clear space equal to the height of the "M" in the wordmark around all sides of the logo. Never crowd it with other elements.

2

Minimum Size

The full logo should never appear smaller than 120px wide in digital contexts, or 30mm wide in print. Below this, use the icon mark only.

3

Never Alter the Logo

Do not stretch, rotate, recolour, add effects, or recreate the logo. Always use the approved SVG files provided in this kit.

4

Background Contrast

Use the Primary logo on white or light surfaces. Use the Reverse (white) logo on dark, navy, or gradient backgrounds. Never place the logo on a busy photo without a solid backing.

5

No Unauthorised Lockups

Do not pair the Momentify logo with partner logos, icons, or taglines without explicit approval from the brand team.

6

Gradient Integrity

The icon's gradient must always run from Cyan (#0CF4DF) to Deep Blue (#1F3395). Do not swap gradient directions or substitute colours.
